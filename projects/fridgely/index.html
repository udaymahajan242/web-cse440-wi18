Meet Fridgely.

Waste less, Spend less and Go green!

Fridgely helps college students on a budget to manage their groceries and prevent waste by maintaining grocery inventories, creating smart lists and suggesting recipes.

Create smart grocery lists.

Create grocery lists with the app manually or automatically based on your inventory.

Track Fridge inventory.

Manage your inventory right within the app and sync it wirelessly with your smart fridge.

Explore Amazing Recipies

Make amazing new dishes based on your inventory thereby wasting less.
